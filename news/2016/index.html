2016 IEEE CIS Summer School on
Computational Intelligence in Big Data Analysis and Robotics

IEEE 计算智能学会暑期研修班 “大数据分析与机器人”

2016年7月4日-6日   四川大学

Introduction

The summer school will provide an attractive opportunity for senior undergraduate students, graduate students and young researchers to catch up with the fundamental theories, algorithms of neural networks models and other CI methods, and also their latest developments and advance in big data analysis and robotic applications. Big data analysis and robotics are of significance to both academic and industrial fields of information technology, computer science, electrical and electronic engineering.

This summer school will deliver the elementary concepts and the state of the art techniques of evolutionary algorithms, multi-objective optimization, adaptive dynamic programming, neural networks, and their inspirations to applying to big data analysis and robotics fields. Through this summer school, we will meet multiple targets including: (1) promoting the research on computational intelligence (evolutionary algorithms, neural networks, adaptive programming, etc) and their applications in big data analysis and robotics in China, (2) serving existing CIS members, (3) promoting CIS and attracting new members, and (4) attracting industry participants.
