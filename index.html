We research falls in the two interplay fields: neural computation and machine learning. More specifically, our primary research interests is to develop brain-style intelligence by modeling neural systems and cognitive computation, and thus to implementing brain based computing in neuromorphic circuits. By engineering neural circuits of the brain, We also intrigued to develop innovative brain-based devices and neuro-cognitive robot. We also interested in general areas of computational intelligence, autonomous and intelligent systems, dynamic systems and control.

Currently, We leading the cognitive computing group and our aim is to develop theoretical and practical solutions to cognitive computation systems, including intelligent memory systems, sensors and neuro-cognitive robot, etc. Our primary research interests include:

  • Robust and precise sensory information encoding by neural circuits.
  • Memory representation, storage and retrieval in neocortex and hippocampus.
  • Synaptic learning mechanisms for learning, short-term and long-term memory.
  • Neuro-Cognitive Robots.
