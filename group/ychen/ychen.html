Biography

I am a lecturer in the College of Computer Science at Sichuan University (SCU), China.

I did my PhD study in the Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University (AAU) from September 2009 to March 2013, under the supervision of Dr. Thomas D. Nielsen. Afterwards, I stayed in the Knowledge and Data Engineering research cluster, Queen's University Belfast, UK and THINC Lab, Georgia University, US, as post-doctoral research associates, respectively.

I obtained my Bachelor and Master degrees from Ningbo Inistitute of Technology, Zhejiang University and University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, in 2006 and 2009, respectively.

Research Interests

I am interested in Artificial Intelligence (in particular, multiagent sequential decision making under uncertainty) and Formal Methods (in particular, machine learning-based model checking).
